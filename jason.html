Broadest Overview map of accidents data

The map below shows hit and run hot spots in the city for the last three years. Major hot spots include down town, out west towards Oak park and also at major traffic intersections.
As can be seen by the heat map hit and runs are much more common at major intersections presumably because stationary traffic are more likely to be hit by drivers attempting to get around turning vehicles and oncoming traffic.
Fatalities for the last three years are an optional data. Only 418 accidents with fatalities were recorded in the city. Or about 100 a year. As opposed to other traffic data, such as hit and runs or the covid correlation, fatal accidents are more sporadic and dispersed. The small sample size makes it harder to glean overall trends.
